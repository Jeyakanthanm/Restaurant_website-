Description of CHICKEN BIRYANI.This is one of the most popular and favourite dish all over the world. It is one of the most loved rice and meat dish that originated from Indian sub-continent.Traditional chicken dum biryani which has a fragrance which is so inviting is made by layering rice and marinated chicken that is flavoured with whole spices, herbs like mint and coriander leaves, curd and ghee. Every household has their unique way of making this dish

CHICKEN CHETTINAD GRAVY

Description of CHICKEN CHETTINAD GRAVY. a spicy chicken gravy from the Chettinad region. This is South India's most popular chicken dish cooked in Chettinad masalas. Chettinad chicken gravy is the most ordered dish in restaurants and popular dish all over south india. Tender pieces of chicken cooked in aromatic Chettinad spices. This is an Authentic Chettinad chicken dish with taste, flavour and colour. This goes well with rice, ghee rice, roti or paratha.

ENNAI KATHIRIKAI KULAMBU

Description of ENNAI KATHIRIKAI KULAMBU.Ennai kathirikai kulambu is one of the most popular South Indian kulambu recipe made with small variety of brinjals, onions, tamarind and spices. Brinjal is cooked with shallots, garlic and roasted masala and simmered in tamarind juice until a layer of oil floats on top. This dish is best served with rice.

About Us

Welcome to **JU's Restaurant**, the best place to enjoy authentic Indian cuisine in Dindigul. We are a family-owned and operated restaurant that has been serving our loyal customers since 2020.
At JU's Restaurant, we believe that food is not just a necessity, but a celebration of life. That's why we use only the freshest ingredients, the finest spices, and the most traditional recipes to create dishes that are rich in flavor, aroma, and texture. Whether you are looking for a hearty meal, a light snack, or a sweet treat, we have something for everyone.
Our menu features a variety of dishes from different regions of India, such as North Indian, South Indian, Mughlai, and Indo-Chinese. You can choose from our signature dishes, such as butter chicken, biryani, dosa, paneer tikka, and more. Or you can try our daily specials, which are prepared with seasonal and local produce. We also have a wide range of vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal options to cater to your dietary needs.
We invite you to join us for a memorable dining experience at Spice Garden. You can dine in our cozy and elegant restaurant, or order online for delivery or takeout. We also offer catering services for parties, weddings, corporate events, and more. No matter the occasion, we will make sure that you and your guests are satisfied and happy.
Thank you for choosing JU's Restaurant, where every bite is a delight!
